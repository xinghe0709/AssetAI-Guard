GENERAL NOTES
This document defines the design loading requirements for the operational deck area,
maintenance access zone, and vessel interface region at Berth 5.
All load limits shall be verified before any heavy equipment deployment.

DESIGN CRITERIA

ITEM: Operational Vehicle Loads (PAGE 2)
- Max Axle Load: 85 t
- Max Wheel Load: 420 kN
- Impact Allowance: 15 %

ITEM: Crane Outrigger Loading (PAGE 2)
- Max Outrigger Load: 1250 kN
- Temporary Setup Load: 980 kN
- Minimum Bearing Plate Size: 1.5 m x 1.5 m

ITEM: Storage and Uniform Loads (PAGE 3)
- Uniform Distributed Load: 35 kPa
- Maintenance Access Load: 12 kPa
- Laydown Area Load: 20 kPa

ITEM: Vessel Interface Criteria (PAGE 4)
- Maximum Displacement: 72000 t
- Mooring Load: 450 kN
- Berthing Load: 680 kN

ITEM: Local Restrictions (PAGE 4)
- Heavy vehicle turning shall be restricted to marked zones only.
- Crane outriggers shall not be placed within 2 m of trench covers.
- Deck load verification is mandatory before vessel service operations.
